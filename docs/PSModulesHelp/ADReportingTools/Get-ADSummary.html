linkGet-ADSummary

linkSYNOPSIS

Get a summary report of your AD domain and forest.

linkSYNTAX

1linkGet-ADSummary [[-Identity] <String>] [-Server <String>] [-Credential <PSCredential>] [<CommonParameters>]

linkDESCRIPTION

This simple command will give you a snapshot-sized summary of your Active Directory domain and forest.

linkEXAMPLES

linkExample 1

1linkPS C:\> Get-ADSummary

12linkGlobalCatalogs    : {DOM1.Company.Pri, DOM2.Company.Pri}
